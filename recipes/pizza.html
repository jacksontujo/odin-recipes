Recipe for Jackson's Pizza

Description

This recipe has been brought down the Tujo bloodline for generations. It will be sure to satisfy your pizza cravings

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Tomatos
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Oregano
  1. Let yeast rise throughout the day
  2. Chop and dice tomatoes prior to heating in pan
  3. Roll dough into a pie shape
  4. Drizzle sauce lightly in a circle
  5. Sprinkle Mozzarella and Oregano onto the pie
  6. Bake for 10 minutes
  7. let sit for 5 minuts
  8. You now have a Jackson pizza!
