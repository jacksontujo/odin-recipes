Recipe for Jackson's Cheeseburger

Description

This recipe has been brought down the Tujo bloodline for generations. It will be sure to satisfy your cheeseburger cravings

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • American Cheese
  • Your choice of toppings
  1. Take a handful of ground beef and mold it into a patty shape
  2. Place the patty on the grill
  3. add slice of cheese as beef turns brown
  4. Grill until brown, but not too dark
  5. Place hamburger onto a bun
  6. Add toppings as you wish
  7. You now have a Jackson burger
