Recipe for Jackson's Spaghetti

Description

This recipe has been brought down the Tujo bloodline for generations. It will be sure to satisfy your pasta cravings

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Prego Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  1. Set a large pan of water to boil
  2. Once the water is boiling, break a handful of spaghetti noodles in half and place them in pan
  3. Add a drizzle of olive Oil
  4. Stir occassionally, let pasta cook until no longer stiff
  5. Drain the Noodles
  6. Place a ton of prego in the empty pan
  7. Mix in the noodles
  8. Add small amount of parmesan cheese
  9. You now have Jackson Pasta!
